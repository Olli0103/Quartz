# Quick Capture

Quick Capture lets you jot down a thought in seconds without creating a new note or navigating away from the Dashboard.

## How it works

1. Click in the Quick Capture text field on the Dashboard
2. Type your thought
3. Press **Enter** or click the send button

Your text is appended to today's **Daily Note** as a timestamped bullet:

```markdown
- 2:34 PM: Your captured thought here
```

## Daily Note

The Daily Note is named `Daily Note YYYY-MM-DD.md` and is created in the root of your vault. If it already exists, Quick Capture appends to it. If it doesn't, Quartz Notes creates it with a heading:

```markdown
# Daily Note — 2026-03-27

- 2:34 PM: First thought of the day
- 3:15 PM: Another idea captured
```

## Tips

- Use Quick Capture for fleeting ideas you want to process later
- Review your Daily Notes periodically to extract actionable items
- Quick Capture is much faster than creating a new note for small thoughts
