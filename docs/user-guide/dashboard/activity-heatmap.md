# Activity Heatmap

The Momentum heatmap visualizes your writing consistency over time, similar to GitHub's contribution graph.

## Reading the heatmap

- Each small square represents **one day**
- The grid shows the past **26 weeks** (approximately 6 months)
- Columns represent weeks (left = oldest, right = today)
- Rows represent days of the week

## Color intensity

Squares are colored using your app's accent color at varying opacity:

| Intensity | Meaning |
|-----------|---------|
| Very faint | No notes edited that day |
| Light | 1-2 notes edited |
| Medium | Several notes edited |
| Bright | Heavy editing day |

The scale is relative to your personal maximum — your busiest day is always the brightest.

## Hover details

On macOS, hover your mouse over any square to see:
- The exact date
- The number of notes edited that day

This information appears in the label below the heatmap grid.

## What counts as activity

A day is "active" when you modify the content of one or more notes. Simply opening a note without editing it does not count.
