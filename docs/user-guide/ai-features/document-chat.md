# Document Chat

Document Chat lets you have a conversation with AI about the note you're currently editing.

## Opening Document Chat

1. Open a note in the editor
2. Click the **chat bubble** icon in the toolbar
3. The chat panel opens as a sheet

## What the AI knows

Document Chat sends the **full content** of your current note as context. The AI can:

- Answer questions about the note's content
- Summarize sections
- Suggest improvements or expansions
- Help brainstorm related ideas
- Explain complex passages

## Using Document Chat

1. Type your question in the text field
2. Press Enter or click Send
3. The AI responds with streaming text (appears word by word)

Your conversation history is preserved within the session. You can ask follow-up questions that reference earlier responses.

## Inserting AI text into your note

After the AI responds, you can copy any part of the response and paste it into your note.

## Privacy

- Only the current note's content is sent to the configured AI provider
- No other notes, vault data, or personal information is included
- If no AI provider is configured, Document Chat is not available
