### Portfolio Simplification Progress

- Major shift to comprehensive packages replacing modular offerings
  - Replacing 9 fixed package SKUs for Cloud ERP Private with single comprehensive technical operations SKU
  - Sales feedback shows need for simplified packages vs. pick-and-choose model
- Four new comprehensive offerings in development:
  - **Cloud ERP Private** - October release, comprehensive technical operations
  - **SuccessFactors** - Q4 release, functional application management package
  - **Business Data Cloud** - Currently in pricing testing, first derivative pricing model
  - **Integration Suite** - Service design complete, tiered pricing model

### New Service Naming Convention

- Three-tier naming structure established with SAP marketing:
  - **Core offerings**: “Cloud Application Services for \[Solution\]” (e.g., CAS for BTP Integration Suite)
  - **Custom offerings**: “Cloud Application Services \[Solution\] Tailored Edition”
  - **Niche services**: “Cloud Application Services for \[Specific Service\] for \[Solution\]”
- Eliminates confusion with previous naming inconsistencies
- Custom SKUs only for fallback scenarios, not majority business
