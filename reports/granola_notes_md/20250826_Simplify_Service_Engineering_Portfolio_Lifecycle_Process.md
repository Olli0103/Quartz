### Portfolio Process Simplification Feedback

- Process overview includes trigger → evaluation → development → deployment → retirement lifecycle
- Single email submission replaces Jira ticket requirement
- Simplified requirement checklist for service qualification
- Automated roadmap generation from tickets

### Role Structure Concerns

- Too many overlapping roles identified:
  - Portfolio area manager, service offering manager, coach, portfolio lead
  - Commercialization lead, service development lead vs service owner
  - Idea coach role questioned as unnecessary layer
- Service owner should be central to entire lifecycle
  - Currently buried in role hierarchy
  - Should own end-to-end process from idea to retirement/improvement
  - Can pull coaches/support as needed rather than mandatory handoffs
- Multiple roles can be consolidated under single person
- Current industrialization creates silos within the team

### Process Gaps and Next Steps

- Missing continuous improvement cycle for existing services
  - Current process only covers new service development
  - Need built-in mechanism for ongoing portfolio enhancement
- Zero ideas currently flowing through formal process
  - Teams bypass process and go directly to service owners
  - This direct approach actually preferred over additional layers
- Feedback loop exists but needs consistency improvements

- No follow-up call planned unless necessary
