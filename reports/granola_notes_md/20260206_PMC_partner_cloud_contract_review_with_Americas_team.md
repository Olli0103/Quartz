### PMC Business Model Structure

- PMC partners buy licenses from SAP, repackage for end customers
- Americas creates custom solutions (e.g., loan processing for banks)
- End users (like Santander Bank) get packaged solutions
- Partner handles AMS (Application Management Services) for their custom solutions
- Historical material numbers still allow PMC sales for older products
