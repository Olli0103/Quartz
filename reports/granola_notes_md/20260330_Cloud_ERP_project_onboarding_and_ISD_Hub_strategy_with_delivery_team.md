### Business Case & Project Scope

- Strategic context: SAP moving cloud application services delivery to ISD Hub
  - Centralize tools, reports, and deliverables in one location
  - Standardize approach across all CNA delivery units
  - Improve end-user experience with best-in-class service delivery
- Current pain points (based on IBM feedback)
  - No central storage location for deliverables
  - Inconsistent templates across teams
  - Unprofessional appearance to customers
  - Disconnected team approaches
- Solution benefits
  - Same look/feel as other SAP services (Preferred Success, Premium Engagement)
  - Scalable report templates vs manual creation
  - Centralized customer information and activity tracking
