### Multi-System Support Case Enhancement

- SAP Sweden NorthStar program identified critical requirement for support tickets
- Current limitation: single system/tenant per case restricts complex scenarios
  - Multi-system integrations (CM, CRM, ERP combinations)
  - Cases where actual problem system differs from initial assignment
  - Impacts AI accuracy due to unreliable master data connections
- Proposed solution: maintain initial system for SLAs while allowing additional systems
  - Customer/engineer can add systems during case lifecycle
  - Mark “current relevant system” for processing
  - Contractual SLAs remain tied to initial system
- Benefits: better data accuracy, smoother case processing, improved system access management
- Next steps: Bernd Harde leading discovery workshop (remote)
  - Harald and Michelle to participate and nominate additional attendees
  - Address authorization risks and usage frequency data
