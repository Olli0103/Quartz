### Proposed UAM Concept Structure

- Move away from ESP-based roles due to upcoming ServiceNow migration
- Define business roles aligned with CARS organizational structure
  - Supplier Manager role for supplier management dashboard access
  - Delivery Center Manager for relevant areas
  - L2 Manager with broader access permissions
- Role assignment principles:
  - Users should only access areas relevant to their business function
  - Finance data restricted from supplier managers
  - Leadership team gets comprehensive access
- Integrate with AIM (Access Identity Management) for automated provisioning
  - Automatic role removal when employees leave
  - Standard SAP approval workflows
  - 2-day SLA for role approvals
