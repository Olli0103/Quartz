### Dashboard Overview & Access

- Five integrated dashboards replacing old delivery excellence system
  - Delivery Excellence: IRT, solution time compliance, duration, first-time resolution rates
  - Backlog Analysis: open tickets, aging rates, early warning tickets
  - Volume Consumption: consumption rates by region, top 10 accounts (ticket/firm contracts only)
  - Internal Audit Control: QATs, process categorization, violation accountability
  - Contract Management: new CAS contracts, ASM nominations, engagement backlogs
- User access management concept in development by Oliver’s team
  - Full access: CAS managers, DC heads, service center teams
  - Cascading permissions based on role relevance
  - Contract management initially internal only, expanding to ASMs/DC heads later

### Key Features & Documentation

- Monthly updates (except early warning tickets - weekly)
- Export functionality for all data tables
- Color-coded thresholds (hover for definitions)
- Two documentation sources:
  - PMF main pages with dashboard overviews, audience, limitations
  - Detailed PowerPoint with KPI calculations and definitions
- New data analyzer tool enables custom views and personal analysis
- Known limitation: Volume consumption missing outcome-based contracts

### Phase 2 Roadmap

- Planned additions:
  - Renewal dashboard (pending reliable data)
  - Outcome-based contracts integration
  - ESP contract tracking automation
  - End date monitoring for renewal activities
- Access rollout requires finalized user management concept
- Timeline: potentially by end of week, official rollout to follow
