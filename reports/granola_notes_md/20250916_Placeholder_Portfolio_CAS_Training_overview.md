### Cloud Application Services Portfolio Overview

- Comprehensive application management for SAP cloud solutions post-implementation
- 1,000+ customers across 70+ countries with 24/7 delivery from 4 global centers
- Predictable outcomes at fixed annual subscription fees
- Focus areas:
  - Cloud ERP Private (main market)
  - Success Factors HCM
  - Customer Experience solutions
  - Business Technology Platform
  - SAP Analytics Cloud

### Service Delivery Structure

- Day-to-day operational improvements with hands-on experience
- Core services span multiple dimensions:
  - Application management (incident/problem resolution, change management)
  - Business AI operations support
  - Advanced security and compliance
  - Performance management
  - Data lifecycle management
  - Test management
- Operates above SAP technical operations layer (infrastructure, OS, database)

### Cloud ERP Private Offerings

- Three commercial variants:
  - PCE - Standard private cloud core ERP scenarios
  - Tailored option - Flexible landscapes for customer-specific needs
  - Enterprise agreement - Commercial construct with component flexibility
- Two service types:
  - Fixed packages: SAP price list items, web shop-style selling
  - Custom scope: Requires configuration/pricing via CAS Solution Center

### Contract Framework & Documentation

- Services co-termed with underlying cloud contracts (typically 5 years)
- Auto-renewal standard, considered cloud revenue and renewal base
- Documentation hierarchy:
  - Customer presentations in SAP document library
  - Scope documents on [sap.com](http://sap.com) with detailed RACI matrices
  - Service repository accessible via CAS Wiki portal
- Fixed packages have defined usage limits (e.g., 24 service requests annually)
- Custom scope requires CAS attachment specifying selected items and limitations

### Legacy Services Transition (July 1 Changes)

- CAS now fully responsible for services previously managed by ECS:
  - Application operations (24 service requests/year)
  - Core performance management
  - Enhanced Managed Service (EMS) for legacy HANA Enterprise Cloud
- EMS characteristics:
  - Time & material basis with prepaid monthly hours
  - Rollover capability (unused hours carry forward)
  - Same technical scope as application operations but hourly charging
  - \~2,500 customers remaining until 2026-2027
- Engagement management transitioning from CDMs to ASMs regionally

### Information Sources & Self-Enablement
