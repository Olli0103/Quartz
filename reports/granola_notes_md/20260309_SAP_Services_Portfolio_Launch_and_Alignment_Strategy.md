### New Portfolio Structure & Launch Status

- External launch completed March 3rd with “Success Unleashed” webinar
- Portfolio now live with three main components:
  - Success Plans (Foundation, Advanced, Max)
  - Professional Services
  - Development Services
  - Application Management
- Single source of truth established: Service & Support Portfolio catalog
  - Replaces previous fragmented documentation
  - All SKU definitions and entitlements now centralized
  - Work Zone Wiki to be phased out this quarter

### Success Plans Scope & Boundaries

- Clear guardrails established for what Success Plans cover vs don’t cover
  - Functional and technical assistance only
  - No execution work (that’s Professional Services or Application Management)
  - No customer-specific customizations
  - Non-production environments only
- Context determines service type:
  - Configuration in Success Plans = feature activation context
  - Configuration in Professional Services = implementation context
  - Configuration in Application Management = continuous operations context
- Missing detailed comparison documentation causing field confusion
  - Sales teams accidentally positioning Success Plans as AMS replacement
  - Delivery teams creating conflicting activity lists

### Application Management Portfolio Evolution

- Vertical products by solution area (Cloud ERP, SuccessFactors, BTP, etc.)
- Horizontal capabilities cross-solution (monitoring, security, performance, data management)
- Enhanced services from ECS being integrated
  - Private cloud operational services
  - Concur managed services transitioning to SAP price list
- Definition: Any managed service for continuous operation/lifecycle management not included in foundational Success Plan

### Service Ownership Structure Changes

- Two distinct ownership roles:
  1. Product owners - vertical solution-oriented commercial units
  2. Capability owners - horizontal cross-solution services
- Matrix collaboration required between vertical and horizontal owners
- Both roles maintain scope/roadmap ownership but different focus areas:
  - Product owners: commercialization, external positioning
  - Capability owners: delivery procedures, technical execution details

### Cross-Portfolio Alignment Initiative

- Focus sessions starting between Advanced and Max Success Plan teams
- Service value maps being created for key areas:
  - Business AI
  - Security
  - Regression testing
  - Data management
- Goal: End-to-end customer experience where Success Plan recommendations automatically trigger execution in entitled services
- Current challenge: Service catalog content incomplete for Max Success Plans

### Next Steps & Actions

- All service owners: Transition content from Work Zone to Service Catalog + SharePoint by end of quarter
- Enhanced ECS services: Define catalog representation and landing pages
- Professional Services and Development Services alignment sessions to be scheduled
- Cross-service collaboration platform needed for workforce-level coordination
- Detailed portfolio comparison documentation to address field confusion
