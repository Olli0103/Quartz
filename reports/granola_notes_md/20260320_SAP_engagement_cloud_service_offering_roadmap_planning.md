### SAP Engagement Cloud Proposition for CAS

- SAP Engagement Cloud (formerly MRCs) rebranded 3 weeks ago
  - SAP Marketing Cloud being discontinued by 2028
  - Clients transitioning to Engagement Cloud Enterprise Edition
- Proposed CAS services:
  1. Application management (incident, problem, change requests)
  2. Advanced security and compliance (user/role management)
- Release support clarification needed
  - Avoid “release guidance” terminology (conflicts with Success Plans)
  - Position as “release impact management” or “successful release support”
  - Focus on hands-on execution vs. guidance
- Technical configuration scope should be downplayed
  - Emphasize hands-on system work as key differentiator

### Market Validation & Pricing Challenges

- Client base analysis: 599 new clients (2022-2026), nearly 100% renewal rate
- Tier-based segmentation excludes Tier 1-2 (ACV too low)
- 62 CAS-eligible clients identified for 2026
  - Pricing at 15% ACV uplift per Arndt’s guidance
  - €51,000 average package = \~€4,000/month
- Significant concerns raised:
  - Pricing too low for comprehensive service delivery
  - Success Factors/Commerce Cloud typically 80-250% uplift
  - Business case insufficient with only 6 expected customers (10% of eligible)
- Data reliability issues:
  - SAP has only 1/3 of total MRCs revenue data
  - Legal integration pending until June 2026
  - Forecasting extremely difficult without complete picture

### Security & Compliance Scope Refinement

- Focus on high-value activities only
  - Role creation/modification where customers lack expertise
  - Technical user management
  - Authorization reviews and updates
- Exclude basic end-user management
  - Not cost-effective for either party
  - Customer responsibility for standard user operations
- Align with Erica (capability owner) on consistent approach across solutions
