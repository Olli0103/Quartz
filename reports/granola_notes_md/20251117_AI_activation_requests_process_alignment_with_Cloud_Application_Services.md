### Cloud Application Services Process Structure

- AI activation embedded within NorthStar success plans (Preferred, Advanced, Max)
- No separate AI activation process - handled through existing ITSM framework
- Service delivery model:
  - Continuous managed service with various tiers
  - All requests flow through structured ticket system
  - No unmanaged customer touchpoints
- Key constraints:
  - Cannot do advisory work (breaks portfolio complementarity)
  - Focus on hands-on execution only
  - Different from consulting-focused success plans
- Budget structure: Fixed engagement with open ticket buckets
- Building collaborative workflows with other teams (ECS, TQM) through engagement managers
