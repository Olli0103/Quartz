### New SAP CAS for BTP Integration Suite Service

- Dedicated service for SAP Integration Suite (separate from existing CAS for BTP)
  - Fixed scope, outcome-based pricing
  - HarmonyReady service - can add to any BOM
  - Response to sales team requests and PIPO end-of-maintenance migration
- Unlimited tickets included (standard and non-standard operations)
  - Configuration and administration of integration suite
  - Standard integrations setup
  - Monitoring and incident/problem management
  - Adaptions and changes to existing iFlows
- Unique iFlow testing service for new releases
  - Pre-test existing custom iFlows before automatic cloud updates
  - Can stop new release deployment if issues detected
  - Only available from CAS

### Pricing Structure and Tiers

- Tiered pricing model focusing on first 4 tiers (most price-sensitive)
  - 1 tenant: €35k
  - 2 tenants: €30k each (€60k total)
  - 3-4 tenants: reduced per-tenant pricing
  - 5+ tenants: further reduced flat rate
- Service limitations per pricing tier
  - Up to 100 iFlows per tenant for operations
  - 5 iFlows per tenant for testing service
- Must contract for actual number of customer tenants (including dev/test)

### Cost Comparison vs In-House Operations

- Customer self-management resource requirements
  - 2 FTEs for 8x5 support model (1-5 tenants)
  - 4 FTEs for 24x7 business-critical support
  - Estimated annual cost: \~€300k for 24x7 coverage
- CAS service cost for 4 tenants: €110k annually
  - Saves approximately two-thirds of operational costs
  - Provides 24x7 expert coverage from day one

### Service Positioning vs Other SAP Services

- CAS vs Preferred Success differentiation
  - Preferred Success: enablement and teaching customers how to operate
  - CAS: direct operational management on customer’s behalf
  - CAS touches productive systems directly
- Single point of contact eliminates ticket ping-pong
  - CAS determines if issue can be resolved directly or needs escalation
  - Forwards tickets to correct SAP components when needed

### Shared Responsibility Model Context

- SAP provides BTP platform availability and functionality
- Customer responsible for applications, configurations, and operations
- CAS bridges gap by taking over operational responsibilities
  - 24x7 expert pool availability
  - Current technology skills maintenance
  - Faster project ramp-up with immediate operational support
