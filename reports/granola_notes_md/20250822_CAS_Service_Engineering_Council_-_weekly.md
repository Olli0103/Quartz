### SAP Sustainability Control Tower Service Proposal

- Ajahan presented CAS service opportunity for Sustainability Control Tower (SCT)

  - One of most sold solutions in SAP’s sustainability management suite
  - 340+ live customers globally (43% partner-implemented)
  - Cloud-based, ERP-centric solution with built-in SAP system integrations
  - Similar architecture to IBP with control tower functionality

- Current market challenges identified:

  - Weak adoption post-implementation
  - Only 2 partners offer managed services
  - Customers lack capability to expand usage
  - Low sustainability priority in most organizations

- Value proposition for CAS:

  - Application management for integrations (inbound/outbound)
  - Functional support for standard and custom metrics
  - Ongoing monitoring and maintenance
  - Enable customer expansion through ticket management framework

### Commercial Structure and Delivery

- Proposed standalone SKU following solution hierarchy:

  - Current: BTP → BDC → Analytics → Sustainability Control Tower (SKU 8014354)
  - Temporary pilot approach: Use generic AM under SKU 8005721 (BTP)
  - Long-term: Dedicated SCT SKU matching full product hierarchy

- Pricing model aligned with SAP’s tiered structure:

  - Three tiers based on active users
  - T-shirt sizing approach (small/medium/large)

- Delivery partnerships being explored:

  - MEE: NTT capabilities (via Babette/Timmu)
  - China: Capgemini interest confirmed (via Hao)

### Implementation Strategy and Support

- Pilot approach starting with APAC market:

  - Highest SCT subscriber concentration
  - Direct connection to 3 customers needing AMS support
  - Local implementation team already providing ad-hoc support

- Product team collaboration confirmed:

  - Ralph Schupp (SCT Product Owner) committed to quarterly governance
  - Valerie (Customer Adoption) very positive on partnership
  - Road map updates and enhancement discussions planned

- Risk considerations noted:

  - No current CAS experience with SCT solution
  - Post-project support always risk driver
  - Need to verify business case against SI partner competition
