### Customer Satisfaction Survey Changes

- Complete quarterly/half-yearly CSAT survey cancelled
  - Quality team demanded 10 questions vs planned 5 simple questions
  - Required experience and service questions beyond scope
  - Decision against holistic approach for now
- Alternative: Wiki-based customer satisfaction reporting dashboard
  - Uses existing ticket-end survey (“How satisfied with SAP service?” 1-5 scale)
  - Only 4% participation rate currently (vs 21% for product support)
  - Limited to SAP-for-me tickets only (excludes Solution Manager, SBC integrations)
  - Dashboard analyzes follow-up questions: solution accuracy, timeliness, resolution time, professionalism, product experience

### Dashboard Implementation Plan

- Enterprise analytics dashboard with ASM customization required
  - One-time setup: delete flags, apply component filters for XXAMS
  - Bookmark functionality for quick future access
  - Can create customer-specific bookmarks or combined view
- Rollout strategy:
  - KT sessions in ASM community calls
  - Focus on bringing feedback into monthly customer discussions
  - Encourage customers to complete surveys to improve response rates
- Regional concerns raised:
  - High-touch customers: ASMs already have weekly contact, question value-add
  - Low-touch/VASM customers: potentially more useful for tracking
  - ServiceNow integration customers (Votorin, others): no survey capability, no alternative allowed
