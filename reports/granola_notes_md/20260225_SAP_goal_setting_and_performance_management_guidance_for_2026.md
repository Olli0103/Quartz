### Goal Setting 2026 Overview

- Performance management session led by Johanna Grave from performance team
- Focus on team-level goal calibration before individual goal setting
- March 31 deadline for all performance goals in system
- Up to 5 performance goals, up to 3 development goals recommended

### Strategic Alignment Process

- L1 strategic/transformational goals already added to everyone’s goal plans
- Manager role: translate strategic goals into meaningful team context
- Individual goals must be individualized - not left untouched from L1 cascades
- Goals are dynamic and adjustable throughout year, not locked after March 31

### Team Calibration Sessions

- Recommended 60-90 minute team workshop format
- Three-part structure:
  1. Explain L1 goals and success indicators
  2. Translate strategic intent into team outcomes/deliverables
  3. Calibrate professional standards expectations within team context
- Toolkit available with facilitator guide and AI prompts
- Document outcomes in notes section of goal forms

### Goal Quality Requirements

- Four key elements unchanged from 2025:
  1. Strategic alignment to broader priorities
  2. Clear success indicators for “successfully achieved” and “partially achieved”
  3. Goal weighting totaling 100% across all goals
  4. Feedback mechanisms and frequency alignment
- Focus on ambitious but achievable goals creating impact, not just effort
- Use AI prompts in goal setting guides instead of static examples
