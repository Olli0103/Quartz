### ICP Role Integration Requirements

- Need dedicated ICP role for Private Cloud ERP service
- Focus on Enterprise Support service integration
  - Data Value Management services
  - Performance optimization
  - Security assessments
- Current gap: Cars team lacks visibility into Enterprise Support reports and recommendations

### Role Options Analysis

- **Option 1: Existing Support Team relationship chain**
  - Leverage current ICP structure (Customer → Support Team → assigned roles)
  - Similar to TQM/ECS role assignments
  - Faster implementation, used by all other teams
- **Option 2: Custom Cars relationship chain**
  - Create “Customer has Cars attached” relationship
  - Include validity periods tied to Cars contract duration
  - More complex, longer implementation time
- **Recommended approach**: Use existing Support Team chain with Cars Lead Consultant role

### Current Process Challenges

- Enterprise Support operates as separate internal entity
- Reports go directly to customers instead of implementation teams
- Cars teams must request reports from customers (inefficient)
- Competing standards between Enterprise Support and Cars teams
- Customer receives reports they cannot implement themselves
