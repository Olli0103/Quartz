### ACR Process Overview & Strategy Shift

- Merit focus concentrated on T1-T3 levels
  - Pay increases most relevant for early career employees
  - Long-term incentives (LTIs) for T4+ levels
- Transition period adjustments
  - Small merit increases still visible for T4-T5 this year
  - LTI vesting changed from 6 to 12 months, then quarterly
  - 3-year vesting period maintained

### Budget Structure & Regional Differences

- Two separate budget categories
  1. Global budget (merit increases + LTIs rest of world)
  2. Germany budget (merit increases + LTIs Germany)
  - No transfers between budgets allowed
- Merit baseline varies by region based on market conditions
- LTI calculation changed significantly
  - No longer percentage of base salary/TCC
  - Individualized by T-level and region
  - Different amounts based on employee location

### LTI Program Changes (Now “Elevate”)

- Rebranded from LTIs to “Elevate”
- Major shift in calculation methodology
  - Region-specific amounts
  - T-level specific amounts
  - Employees cannot calculate in advance
- Important communication point for managers explaining changes to reports
