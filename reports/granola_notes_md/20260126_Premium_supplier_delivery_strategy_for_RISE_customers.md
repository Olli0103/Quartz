### Enabling Premium Supplier Delivery

- Proposal to allow Control-S end-to-end delivery of Cloud ERP packages
  - They already deliver 85% of foundation package components
  - No escalations historically with premium supplier setups
  - Would eliminate deal cycle disruption when CAS unavailable
- Major concerns raised:
  - Control-S reputation issues in Middle East market
  - Loss of customer control and governance oversight
  - SLA accountability complications in shared environments
  - No consumption visibility or influence mechanisms
- Risk assessment:
  - Current €6M exposure manageable
  - Opening floodgates could create €30M+ exposure without controls
  - IBM precedent shows consumption challenges
