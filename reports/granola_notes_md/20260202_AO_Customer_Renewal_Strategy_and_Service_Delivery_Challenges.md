### AO Service Crisis & Renewal Strategy

- Critical consumption statistics revealed
  - 539 AO customers worth €16M total
  - Average 9% utilization rate across customer base
  - Majority spending <€100K (Foundation migration not viable)
  - Customers consuming >10 tickets annually barely visible in data
- Renewal risk analysis
  - 55 customers renewing this year, projected to lose all without intervention
  - AO packages doubled between 2024-2025 despite being removed from price list
  - 5-7 years of existing contracts still eligible for renewal
- Proposed intervention strategies
  - Move recurring service requests from ESP to SPC
  - Create proactive recurring tickets for customers
  - Implement service plans similar to old EMS model
  - 30-day opt-out policy for automatic service execution
  - Target 20% customer retention improvement (from 9% to 30% consumption)
