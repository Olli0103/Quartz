### SAP Leadership Update & Market Context

- Thomas von Allen (COO, 25-year SAP veteran) joined as guest speaker
- Stock performance concerns due to AI/SaaS market assumptions
  - Investors placing SAP in “SaaS penalty box”
  - Market undervaluing SAP’s AI-resistant business model
  - Need to prove market wrong through cloud adoption and business AI adoption
- Strategic focus: seamless customer experience across delivery teams
  - Bringing P&A, GCS, and ASC teams together under Global Delivery Hub
  - Following CAS model for engagement with success plans

### 2025 Performance Review

- Revenue progression shows strategic validation
  - Public cloud revenue: 15.7M → 17.6M (first growth since reintroduction)
  - Private cloud: 16% growth (first double-digit growth in years)
  - On-premise business: 15.1M → 0.4M (expected sunset)
- Top 10 customers by revenue identified
- New bookings include major wins like Iberdrola
- Team achievements despite organizational challenges
  - BDMs delivered with reduced team size
  - Solution center maintained and grew capacity
  - All regional delivery teams contributed

### Critical Challenges Identified

- Consumption rates at 60-65%, target 80%+
  - Many customers not consuming fixed packages already purchased
  - Primary focus area for 2026
- Renewal churn significant concern
  - “Big hole in bottom of pipe” - substantial revenue loss
  - Three main causes identified:
    1. Customers moving away from underlying solution areas
    2. Conversions (e.g., AMS on HEC → Rise)
    3. Direct CAS cancellations while keeping solution
- Customer satisfaction currently 4.2, targeting 4.5

### 2026 Strategic Priorities

- Five key focus areas with aggressive metrics:
  1. Delivering proactive outcomes as one team
  2. 80%+ consumption rate
  3. 95%+ net revenue retention (NRR)
  4. 4.5+ customer satisfaction
  5. 200%+ ROI methodology development
- Mindset shift required:
  - From siloed entitlements to integrated customer outcomes
  - From incident solving to proactive value demonstration
  - From technical metrics to business value reporting
- Cross-SAP collaboration expansion
  - Systematic engagement with Advanced/Max Success Plans
  - Professional services integration across all accounts
