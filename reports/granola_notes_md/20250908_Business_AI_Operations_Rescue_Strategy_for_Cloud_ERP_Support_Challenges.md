### Cloud ERP Business AI Operations Service Discussion

### Current Customer Support Challenges

- Large enterprise customers experiencing prolonged troubleshooting processes
  - Levi’s: Daily 30-minute calls scheduled all week
  - 24 participants per troubleshooting call (mix of AI Rig, development, AI Factory teams)
  - Incidents passed between teams causing customer frustration
- Mahindra case: Similar extended troubleshooting scenario
- Common issues include:
  - Azure private cloud backend connectivity problems
  - Cloud Connector configuration errors
  - Work Zone setup failures
  - Role replication/synchronization issues

### Existing Service Limitations

- AI Factory activation service scope:
  - Limited to non-production system activation only
  - Additional systems (quality, pre-prod, production) require separate commercial offerings
  - Only valid for greenfield implementations
  - Cannot be used when customers have existing cloud identity services or work zones
- Partner implementers (Deloitte, Accenture) often lack complete technical knowledge
  - Frequently misconfigure systems
  - Create downstream issues requiring SAP intervention

### Proposed “SEAL Team” Service Model

- Quick response specialized team for rescue scenarios
- Target situations:
  - Customer attempts self-implementation after seeing AI Factory demo
  - Partner implementer failures requiring immediate resolution
  - Alternative to lengthy SAP support ticket process
- Team composition: Experts across all relevant domains (BTP, S/4HANA, Work Zone, Cloud Connector, etc.)

### Key Implementation Concerns (Bargav’s Requirements)

- Contractual feasibility within CAS delivery model
  - Team measured on utilization, revenue, booking KPIs
- Pipeline sizing requirements:
  - Need concrete customer volume projections
  - Partner capacity planning considerations
- Boundary definition challenges:
  - Multiple stakeholders: Service implementers, premium engagement teams, product support
  - Risk of becoming additional participant in existing 24-person calls
  - Scope limitations (e.g., BTP/S4HANA vs Azure/AWS integration issues)

- Pascal: Draft boundary conditions proposal defining service scope limits
