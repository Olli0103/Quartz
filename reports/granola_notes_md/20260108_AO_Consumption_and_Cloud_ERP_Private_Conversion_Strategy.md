  - BTP Corps: Named delivery team with unlimited tickets
  - AO: Works via service request app with automatic backend processes

  - Entitlement switching in ESP
  - Service request routing to delivery teams
- Customer messaging complexity
  - No transparency on ticket consumption rationale
  - Difficult to justify 20 vs 40 vs 80 ticket consumption for specific services
