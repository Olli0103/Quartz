- IBM feedback (started September):

### Current Workaround Solutions

- CloudALM projects created for IBM transparency

- ASM team creating individual solutions
  - Each ASM developing own tracking methods
  - No standardized approach across customers
  - Creative but inconsistent delivery experience

### Proposed CloudALM for Service Solution

  - Customer readiness documentation and videos
  - Clear expectations before service kickoff
  - Centralized scope and requirements tracking
- Delivery standardization:
  - Templates for all packages across customers
  - Automated reporting replacing manual PowerPoints
  - Direct integration between internal and customer tenants
  - Issues and recommendations tracked in real-time
- Resource management visibility:
  - Clear package assignments per team member
  - Manager oversight of resource utilization
  - Harmonized approach across all SAP delivery units
