### Information

- Drew AI pilot program launching for consultants to track efficiency improvements in ticket resolution
- Pilot duration: 4 weeks with weekly check-ins scheduled
- Drew AI has access to community content, KBAs, SAP notes, and learning materials
- Tool accessible via ticket menu (More → Drew for consultants) or direct browser link
- Excel tracking sheet created to measure time saved: comparing task completion time with and without AI
- Cheat sheet developed with example prompts for:
  - Incident management (triage, action plans)
  - Problem management (root cause analysis)
  - Implementation plans for change management
- Expert Workspace feature available for uploading customer-specific documentation
  - Teams can share custom AI experts with uploaded files
  - Enables questions on customer-specific guidelines and procedures
- Drew AI uses specialized model trained on SAP content (not bleeding edge but grounded to SAP ecosystem)
- Teams channel created for ongoing communication and document sharing

### Decision

- Consultants will serve as key contacts and community members for broader AI rollout
- Mandatory documentation of all AI usage cases in provided Excel sheet
- Focus on cases where AI provides measurable time savings, especially for research-heavy tasks like RCA

### Action

- Consultants to use Drew AI actively throughout daily work, not just occasionally
- Document each case in Excel sheet with:
  - Case details and ticket links
  - Time estimation without AI vs with AI
  - Task type (incident, change, problem, application management, basis)
- Weekly 20-minute progress meetings scheduled for next 4 weeks
- Oliver to distribute all meeting materials and documentation post-meeting
- Team to test Expert Workspace functionality for customer-specific use cases

### Risk

- Drew AI cannot access live ticket data directly - requires manual copy/paste of ticket content
- Larger tasks may see reduced AI effectiveness due to model limitations
- Prompt engineering skills critical for successful outcomes - inadequate prompting reduces value
- Current SAP AI implementation less flexible than external alternatives for certain technical scenarios
