### Renovation Project Overview

- SAP Labs Dresden DRE04 modernization project officially approved
- Budget secured at €6 million after extensive negotiations with SAP
  - Significant increase from initial allocation
  - Positions Dresden as key development location
  - Investment demonstrates confidence in local teams

### New Workspace Design Concept

- Shift from traditional desk layout to modern hybrid work environment
- Core elements:
  - Individual workstations with height-adjustable desks
  - Shared function spaces (focus pods, meeting rooms, collaboration areas)
  - Centralized coffee area relocated to 5th floor with Zwinger view
  - Gaming room moved to 1st floor for better noise separation
- Acoustic improvements throughout building
  - Sound-absorbing wall panels in team desk areas
  - Modular privacy pods for calls/concentrated work
  - Better sound separation between zones

### Technical Infrastructure Updates

- IT and security systems modernized to latest standards
