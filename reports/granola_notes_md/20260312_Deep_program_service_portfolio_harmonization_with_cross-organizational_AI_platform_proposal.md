### Current Challenge: Siloed Customer Experience

- Customer journey fragmented across multiple SAP organizations
  - Product support tickets → ECS colleagues → Application management
  - Customers ping-pong between different teams for single issues
- Example scenarios:
  - Performance problem requires custom code fix → multiple handoffs
  - SAP note implementation spans product support, ECS, and application services
  - Customer dissatisfaction due to lack of seamless experience
- No end-to-end delivery despite harmonized portfolio across Thomas Fiesta, Stefan Steiner, Peter Blum organizations

### Proposed AI-Powered Solution Architecture

- Leverage existing integrated business data cloud (BD system)
- Connect delivery systems: ServiceNow, SPC, ESP
- AI recommendation engine integration:
  - Checks customer contracts automatically
  - Guides delivery consultants on next steps
  - Prevents sending customers to wrong teams
- Real-time contract validation during ticket resolution
- Seamless handoffs between organizational units
